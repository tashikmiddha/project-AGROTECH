Increase Agriculture Yield

1

Sensor Data Collection

We deploy sensors in the field to collect real-time data including temperature, humidity, soil pH, rainfall, and NPK levels.

2

Dataset Generation

The collected raw data from all sensors is cleaned and formatted to build structured datasets used for model training.

3

Model 1: Crop Prediction

We train a machine learning model to predict the most suitable crop based on current soil and environmental conditions.

4

Model 2: Watering Schedule Prediction

Using weather forecasts and soil moisture data, we predict the optimal irrigation timing and quantity for the selected crop.

5

Model 3: Fertilizer Recommendation

Based on NPK levels, soil type, and the chosen crop, the system recommends the right fertilizer combination for better yield.

6

Model Evaluation & Testing

Each model is tested and validated using unseen data to ensure high accuracy, reliability, and performance in real conditions.

7

Deployment & Monitoring

All models are integrated into a smart dashboard or mobile app for real-time monitoring and decision-making by farmers.
